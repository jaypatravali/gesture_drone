kinect_drone

Controlling the Parrot Ardrone 2.0 with a Kinect Motion sensor and a LeapMotion sensor. Simulation done on the turtlesim package

ROS packages needed: 1. turtlesim 2. skeleton_markers 3. ardrone_autonomy 4. openni_camera 5. turtlesim
